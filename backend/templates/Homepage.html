Frequently Asked Questions

Is TradeWonk secure?

Absolutely. Security is our top priority. We use bank-level encryption (AES-256) for your data, secure connections for linking accounts (often via trusted partners like Plaid), and implement strict internal controls. We never store your brokerage login credentials.

What assets can I track?

TradeWonk supports a wide range of assets including stocks, ETFs, bonds, mutual funds, cryptocurrencies (from major exchanges and wallets), real estate (manual entry), commodities, cash accounts, and more. We are continuously expanding our coverage.

How much does TradeWonk cost?

We offer different pricing tiers, including a free plan with core features! You can find detailed information on our Pricing Page (link to be added later).

How do I connect my accounts?

We partner with secure aggregation services (like Plaid, Yodlee - mention if applicable) to link your accounts safely. You'll log in through their secure portal, and TradeWonk receives read-only access to your transaction and balance data. You can also add assets manually.
